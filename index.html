The Story

Court Wizard started as a hobby project — one developer learning Rust and game development from scratch. What began as a simple hobby with the Bevy engine grew into a potential business venture.

Built entirely in Rust, Court Wizard is a 2D/3D real-time strategy tower-ish defense game where you play as a powerful wizard defending your castle from relentless waves of attackers. Cast devastating spells from your tower, support your defenders, and master the arcane arts to protect your king.

Now the first title from Blackhearth Games, an indie studio born from the belief that a single developer with a little free time can build something that perhaps the rest of the world would enjoy. Open source. Made with passion for fantasy and magic. Licensed under GPL v3.0.

Features

11 Wizard Archetypes

From the Rune Caster to the Meteorologist. Yes, one of them even uses poop magic.

32 Spells & Talent Trees

9 talents per spell across 3 tiers. The math is fine. Trust the wizard.

Roguelite Campaign

25 levels, seeded runs, and difficulty sliders. Blame the RNG.

Endless Mode

Infinitely scaling enemies. Infinitely scaling you. It gets ridiculous.

Boss Fights

Ogre, Lich, Hags, and Dark Mage. They all want you dead. Specifically.

Multiplayer Versus

Peer-to-peer PvP. Bring a friend. Destroy their kingdom instead.
